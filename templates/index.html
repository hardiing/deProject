NFL Gambling Tool

  • Win Trends
  • Against the Spread Trends
  • Over/Under Trends

Data found in each table:


Win Trends: Win/Loss Records, Winning Percentage, Margin of Victory, and Against the Spread +/-


Against the Spread Trends: Against the Spread Record, Cover Percentage, Margin of Victory, and Against the Spread +/-


Over/Under Trends: Over Record, Over Percentage, Under Percentage, and Total +/-


About the project:


A web scraping application to collect multiple tables with data that can be used to study trends in the NFL regarding gambling.


Technologies used: Python, Flask, MySQL, HTML/CSS
